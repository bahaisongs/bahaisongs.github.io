Privacy Policy for Bahá’í Songs

Effective Date: 22-04-2025

Bahá’í Songs ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how the Bahá’í Songs app handles user information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration or login is required.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact lists.
  • We do not request access to your microphone, camera, location, or files.

2. Non-Personal Data

We may collect anonymous usage data via standard mobile analytics tools (e.g., Google Play Console) to help improve the app. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous crash or error reports

This data is not linked to your identity and is used only to improve app performance and stability.

3. Audio Playback

The app provides access to a curated collection of Bahá’í songs. All audio files are stored directly on your device during installation, and playback occurs locally. No streaming is used, and no playback history is stored or transmitted to our servers.

4. Media Delivery and Third-Party Services

The Bahá’í Songs app uses Cloudflare R2, a secure object storage service, to host the audio and media files. These files are downloaded to the device as part of the app installation process.

The app does not stream audio during playback, and no media files are fetched from the internet after installation.

During app updates or when checking for the latest version, the app may access files from Cloudflare R2, in which case:

  • Cloudflare may collect non-identifying metadata (e.g., IP address, device type, timestamp) for security and performance monitoring.
  • This information is handled in accordance with Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy and is not used for advertising or tracking.

We do not collect or store this metadata ourselves.

5. Children's Privacy

The Bahá’í Songs app is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe that such data may have been collected, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. Data Security

While we do not store user data, we take reasonable measures to ensure that the app and the systems we use are secure and protected from misuse, unauthorized access, or disclosure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on our website or within the app. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
